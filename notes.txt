Neural networks are basically giant math equations with millions of variables. If you give a model with 3.4 million parameters only a few thousand images (and a large learning rate), it has so much "brain power" that it doesn't bother learning general rules about what pneumonia looks like. Instead, it just **memorizes** the exact pixels of the training images. That's why your training loss went to 0, but it failed completely on validation images.
